NEWLY BUILT OCEAN VIEW LUXURY ESTATE

Villa Jaguar is a stunning newly constructed masterpiece featuring 4 spacious master bedrooms and 5 luxurious bathrooms. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, this architectural gem showcases the finest interior design and is built to the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Perched in an exclusive location, the villa offers breathtaking ocean views to both the south and north, treating guests to spectacular sunsets from multiple vantage points. The expansive pool area features numerous sitting areas perfect for relaxation, while maintaining complete privacy. Located just minutes from Santa Teresa's vibrant center, yet secluded enough to feel like your own private retreat.
